About Audio Addict

Audio Addict is a dedicated sound engineering studio founded in Kalyan, Maharashtra. We provide a professional and acoustically treated environment for all your audio needs. Led by Vaibhav, an experienced sound engineer, we specialize in delivering high-quality results for local and international clients, both in our studio and through remote freelance work. Our mission is to elevate your sound with precision and passion.
